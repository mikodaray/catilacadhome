Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Is the CEO Systems Method™ right for me?
Yes — if you're a business owner who feels like your business runs on chaos, effort, and guesswork, this was built for you. It doesn't matter what industry you're in, whether you have a team or not, or how long you've been running your business. The 4 systems work for any business at any stage. The earlier you build them, the easier everything becomes.
How long does the course take to complete?
It's designed as a 30-day program at 15–30 minutes per day. But because it's self-paced with lifetime access, you can move faster or slower depending on your schedule. Some people complete it in 2 weeks. Others take 6 weeks and go deeper.
Do I need the course before applying for mentorship?
Not required — but highly recommended. The course gives you the foundation and vocabulary that makes our mentorship sessions far more productive. Many mentorship clients come in as course graduates, which means we skip the basics and go straight into personalized strategy.
What kind of businesses is this for?
Any business owner who wants to stop running their business on effort and guesswork — and start running it on systems. Whether you're a freelancer, coach, consultant, retailer, or any kind of entrepreneur with a team or without. The frameworks work at any stage and for any business type. The systems just look slightly different depending on where you are.
How do I apply for mentorship?
Click the apply button on your preferred mentorship package. You'll be directed to book a discovery call or fill out a short application. Cat personally reviews every application to make sure it's the right fit for both sides.
Is there a refund policy on the course?
Yes — there's a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you go through the material and feel it wasn't worth your investment, reach out and we'll make it right. No complicated process.
