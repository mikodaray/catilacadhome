✍️ Day 7 Prompt — Your Sales System on One Page

  • Who is your ideal client? ("I help ___ who want ___")
  • What is your Hero Offer? ("I offer ___ for ___ who want ___ without ___")

  • How do you structure your sales conversations? (4 steps)
  • What is your retention step after every transaction?

CEO

The Spend System™

The Profit Awareness Shift™

Your Money System on One Page
How you track income · manage expenses · price · support steady cash flow.
🔑 The Busy But Broke Cycle™

Being fully booked does not automatically mean being profitable. Without aligned pricing and tracked expenses, more clients just means more work — not better results. This week breaks that cycle.

✍️ Day 21 Prompt — Your Money System on One Page

  • What is your total income this month?
  • What are your expenses? (fixed, variable, small recurring)
  • What is your actual profit? (Revenue – Expenses)
  • Is your main offer priced sustainably — for you?
  • What one weekly action will you repeat to support consistent income?
Operator
Focuses on how much comes in — relies on feeling — avoids the numbers
CEO
Focuses on what is kept — manages money with clarity and intention
